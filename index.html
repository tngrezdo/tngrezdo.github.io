About Me

Hello I'm Tessa Grezdo and I am currently a senior at the University of San Francisco majoring in Environmental Science. I chose to study in this field because I have an ongoing passion towards environmental health. My goal is help create a more sustainable future for all societies. In order to achieve this, I take every opportunity I can to gain skills and expand my knowledge in Environmental Science. Along with the many courses I have taken involving Environmental Science, I have also been a hydrology research assistant for multiple professors in the Environmental Science department. In addition, I am interning for Schutze and Associates, an environmental consulting firm located in Fremont, CA. When I am not trying to save the world, I enjoy hiking, hula dancing, cycling and just exploring the natural world around us.
